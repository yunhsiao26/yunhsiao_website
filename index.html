About Yun

As a designer who believes in human-centered design, I aim to bring design thinking into businesses to bridge gaps for people. I am also...

Excited about co-working with multi-discipline teams and transforming complex concepts into intuitive and engaging experiences.

Having a great sense of humor and respecting inclusiveness very much.

Taking feedback as a gift for me and my team to improve and grow.

If you are interested to know more about me and my stories, please feel free to contact me at yunhsiao@umich.edu. I look forward to talking with you!

My Tool Kit

Design

I have great passion for human-centered design, and cultivate myself as a full-stack designer specializing in interaction design, information architecture design, information visualization design and graphic/visual design.

Research

I conduct both qualitative and quantitative research in user studies, market research, and design evaluation to involve stakeholder needs and support engaging user experience and implicable design solutions.

Values

With training in both human-computer interaction and library information science, I respect these values and try to adopt them in my design: usability, accessibility, scalability, sustainability, feasibility, and diversity.
